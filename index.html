About Me

I am a results-driven AI/ML Specialist and Data Engineer based in Brisbane, Australia. With extensive experience spanning defense, finance, academia, and public sectors, I've consistently delivered transformative projects that drive innovation and efficiency.

My expertise lies in designing scalable data solutions and modernizing legacy systems using cutting-edge technologies such as Snowflake, AWS Glue, AWS Lambda, and various AI/ML frameworks including TensorFlow and PyTorch. Throughout my career, I've demonstrated success in driving digital transformation initiatives, providing client consulting services, and leading cross-functional teams in agile environments.

Currently serving as a Manager of Data Engineering at Queensland Building and Construction Commission, I oversee the implementation of data warehouse solutions and ETL pipelines, collaborating with stakeholders to align technical solutions with business objectives.

My passion for artificial intelligence and data engineering is complemented by my academic background, holding a PhD in Artificial Intelligence & Image Processing from Central Queensland University, where my research focused on optimized ensemble classifiers. I bring both theoretical knowledge and practical expertise to every project, aiming to create innovative, data-driven decision-making solutions that deliver tangible business value.
